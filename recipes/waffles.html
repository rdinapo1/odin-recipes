Classic Waffles

A lovely, crispy waffle perfect for the morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ cups milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron. Beat eggs in large bowl with hand beater until fluffy.
  2. Beat in flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla, just until smooth.
  3. Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray.
  4. Pour mix onto hot waffle iron. Cook until golden brown. Serve hot.
